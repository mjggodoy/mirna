KHAOS group

The KHAOS research team consists of four faculty members, one postdoctoral researcher and seven PhD students. The Computer Science Department of the University of Málaga is formed by 121 members including full, associate and assistant professors, instructors and fellows. The research KHAOS group is part of the Software Engineering Group (GISUM). Formed in 1990, the main activities of GISUM are related to Software Engineering applied to Distributed Systems. GISUM has been awarded the highest grade of distinction (“group of excellence”) by the Regional Government of Andalusia.

The main research lines are:

  • Data and Knowledge Management in the Semantic Web.
  • Composition and Adaptation of Software Components and Services, including specific topics such as Semantics Web Service Compositon and Semantic ESB.
  • Semantic Web applied to Bioinformatics.
  • Application of Big Data technology.
  • Application of optimization algorithms to solve bioinformatic problems such as molecular docking.
José F. Aldana Montes
José F. Aldana Montes is a full professor at the languages and computing sciences department from the University of Málaga. He has more than 25 years of expertise researching and teaching about databases. His main fields of interest are Semantic MiddleWare, Semantic Data and Application Integration and Database extension using formal semantics. Furthermore, he has developed several works in Bioinformatics field. He has also obtained several EU, national and regional projects related to Bioinformatics.
Ismael Navas Delgado
Ismael Navas Delgado is an assistant professor at the languages and computing sciences department from the University of Málaga. His main interests are Semantic Web, Linked Data, Big Data analysis and their applications in Bioinformatics. He has also published works in high quality journals that apply these technologies to solve problems of integration in Life Sciences.
Esteban López Camacho
Esteban López Camacho is a Ph.D student in the group KHAOS at the University of Málaga. He finished his bachelor of software engineering and the master of Artificial Intelligence and Software Engineering. He has experience in different research topics such as Semantics Web, Linked Data and data integration. He is an expert in software design and has a wide knowledge on programming languages. He has also worked with Optimization algorithms applied to Bioinformatics. His experience has led to different publications in high quality journals.
